The Models

Sarnoff's Law (V ∝ n): Broadcast value. Adding one viewer adds one unit of value.

Odlyzko's Law (V ∝ n log n): Diminishing utility in massive networks.

Metcalfe's Law (V ∝ n²): Telecommunications value. Each participant adds a connection for every existing member.

Reed's Law (V ∝ 2ⁿ): Group-forming value (DAOs/communities). Scales with possible subgroups.
